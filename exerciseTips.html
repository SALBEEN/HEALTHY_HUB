Exercise Tips

Regular exercise keeps your body strong and your mind sharp. Here are simple tips and routines for beginners and daily practice.


Simple Daily Exercises

  • Warm-up: 5–10 minutes of light jogging or stretching.
  • Stretching: Focus on major muscle groups.
  • Cardio: Running, cycling, or brisk walking.
  • Strength Training: Push-ups, squats, planks.
  • Cool-down: Stretch and breathe deeply.
